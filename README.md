# MuscleUp

Ynov Campus Master’s end project.
The objective of this project is to use all the skills learned during our studies along the years.
Developed in SwiftUI.

MuscleUp is a mobile application aimed at making sport more fun and attractive for gamers and competitors.
With a leaderboard and a rank system, you compete with others, but especially yourself.
Try to climb the ladder and surpass yourself and your friends.

The application allows you to record your sessions and track your performance in real time. 
Thanks to a sensory sensor on the weights and machines, the application detects whether you are performing the exercises correctly and automatically records your number of sets and repetitions accordingly.
At the end of your session, you obtain ranking points based on your performance and objectives.
You can therefore follow your progress curve to beat your previous score. You can also compete against other members or your friends, to be the best.
